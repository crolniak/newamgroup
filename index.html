WHO WE ARE

New Amsterdam Group was established by a group of experienced international bankers and lawyers to provide independent wealth management, corporate advisory, and investment services to individuals and families.

Based in New York, NewAm’s team of seasoned highly qualified professionals has expertise in U.S. and International wealth management and transaction services:

- Wealth planning and asset protection

- Strategic asset management, asset allocation, and risk management

- Uniquely positioned to manage issues associated with privately held assets (operating company issues; art; real estate, etc)

- Investment banking, and personal and corporate finance

- Management of complicated domestic and international activities requiring interactions between lawyers, accountants, bankers, and advisors

We have built NewAm so that our interests are completely aligned with those of our clients:

Independent client-centric advice

No conflicts—one source of income; no benefits from product selection other than client returns

Custody and trade execution handled by independent 3rd parties
